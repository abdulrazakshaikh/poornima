About

I have been painting since 1984

It began with expressing my love for the art through oils on canvas, as i found the medium very fluid and easy to work with.

Born in New Delhi, I am based in Mumbai now since over a decade. Since 2019, my 'passionate affair with the brush' transformed from using oils to Acrylics on canvas.

Acrylics lend a brilliant texture on board, are luminous and may be used to paint a splendid array of art forms.

There's also a display of imagination through colors and hues with a lucid depiction of the sense around the work.

I choose to narrate stories through abstract forms of art and have also been currently being doing alot of abstract 'form' play through the depiction of Cityscapes and conceptual art.
